# Brand Brief & Open Questions — Meat Center, Lancaster SC

Intake research date: 2026-06-18. Anchored on phone **803-286-5077**.

---

## Brand / Tone Read

Meat Center reads as an **old-school, no-frills hometown butcher / meat market** —
not a trendy craft-butcher concept. The consistent signal across reviews is
**quality cuts at honest, beat-the-grocery-store prices**, with customers
specifically praising bulk **"box" deals**, weekly specials, a clean and
well-organized store, and friendly, courteous staff. Several reviewers say it's
worth driving in from out of town.

For design and copy: lean **warm, plainspoken, and value-forward** — a trusted
local staple, family-style, community-rooted. Think butcher-paper and red/cream
classic-meat-market cues over minimalist gourmet styling. Avoid over-claiming
craft/artisan language (e.g. dry-aging, house charcuterie) unless the owner
confirms it — those came from a generic directory template, not from the
business.

---

## CRITICAL flag before building anything

There are **two** similarly named Lancaster meat businesses, and the public web
mixes them up:

1. **TARGET — "Meat Center"**, 657 Lancaster Bypass E (Hwy 9 Byp E), phone
   **803-286-5077**. Active; reviews as recent as May 2026.
2. **"The Meat Center" / "Buford Meat Station" / "Buford Meat Center"**, 4457
   Pageland Hwy, phone **803-283-4121**. Listed **CLOSED / out-of-business**
   (Yelp, BBB).

The **Facebook page `facebook.com/TheMeatCenter`** and **Instagram
`@the.meatcenter`** appear tied to business #2 (the closed one). Treat anything
sourced from those pages — including the "Certified Angus Beef, never frozen"
language and the "family owned" claims — as **not confirmed for the target**.

Also note: **Billy Everall**, named by Buzzfile as owner of the 803-286-5077
business, **passed away Dec 22, 2025**, and his obituary calls him "former owner
of The Meat Center." So do **not** name a current owner on the site without
confirmation.

3. Year established — is 1992 correct? (single source)
4. Founding story / family history we can tell?
5. Who currently owns / operates the business? (Billy Everall, listed by
   directories, died Dec 2025.)

**Contact & location**
6. Exact street number — 657, 622, or 662 Lancaster Bypass E? (sources disagree)
7. Business email address?
8. Official Facebook / Instagram / any social accounts? (the ones found may
   belong to the closed Pageland Hwy shop)
9. Parking situation to describe for customers?
10. A landmark / "look for us near ___" line?

**Hours**
11. Saturday closing time — 5 PM or 6 PM? (sources disagree)
12. Any seasonal/holiday hours we should note?

**Products & services** (confirm which are true; none of these are verified)
13. Do you carry/feature **Certified Angus Beef / Prime & Choice**, "cut fresh,
    never frozen"? (claim may belong to the other business)
14. Do you offer **custom cuts**?
15. **Seafood/fish?** **Deli / prepared foods?**
16. **Smoked meats / on-site smoking?**
17. **Holiday or pre-order** programs (hams, turkeys, etc.)?
18. **Freezer / bulk orders** (quarter/half/whole, custom box deals) — details?
19. **House-made sausage** or other specialty items?
20. Any signature or best-selling cuts you want highlighted?

**Pricing**
21. Anything price-related you want shown (e.g. current box-deal lineup), or keep
    pricing off the site?

**Reputation / testimonials**
22. OK to use customer quotes? Confirm the 3 candidate testimonials in
    content.json (wording, names, permission).

**Photos**
23. Can you provide original high-res photos? Needed: storefront/sign, interior,
    the meat case, a bulk box deal, and staff/owner. (All photos found online are
